Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 31, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the Guitar Tuner app uses information.

Microphone Access

The app requests access to your device microphone only to detect the pitch of guitar strings and help tune your instrument.

Audio from the microphone is processed only on your device. The app does not record, save, upload, or share any audio.

Information Collection

The app does not collect, store, or share any personal information.

No account, login, or registration is required to use the app.

Third-Party Services

The app does not use third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Children's Privacy

The app does not knowingly collect any personal information from children.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated in the future. Any changes will be posted on this page.
